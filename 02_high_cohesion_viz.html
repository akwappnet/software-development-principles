üéØ What is High Cohesion?

High cohesion means that elements within a module or component work together toward a common goal. Think of a sports team where everyone knows their role and works together, versus a group of random people trying to play together. High cohesion is like having a specialized kitchen where the prep station only does prep work, the cooking station only cooks, and everything is organized for maximum efficiency.

The Golden Rule: Things that belong together, should stay together. Things that don't belong together, should be separated.
